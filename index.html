About Us

SAI SIVASAKTHI CHARITABLE TRUST

Welcome to SAI SIVASAKTHI CHARITABLE TRUST, an organization dedicated to making a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals and communities. Established in 29th day of January 2022, our trust is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1886, and is based in Naickenpalayam, Coimbatore District.

Our Mission

At SAI SIVASAKTHI CHARITABLE TRUST, our mission is to uplift and empower the underprivileged sections of society through various initiatives and programs. We strive to foster a sense of community, provide essential services, and create opportunities for those in need. Our focus areas include education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and rural development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a society where every individual has access to the resources and opportunities they need to lead a dignified and fulfilling life. We envision a world where communities are self-sufficient, inclusive, and resilient, with equal opportunities for all.
